Bricks 4 Kidz

Education and play for kids, using LEGO®! Workshops, vacation programs and birthday parties.

Bricks 4 Kidz® programs provide an extraordinary atmosphere for students to build unique creations, play games and have fun using LEGO® Bricks.

Our programs are built around our own proprietary LEGO® model plans, designed by in-house engineers and architects, with exciting themes such as space, construction, superheros and amusement parks. At Bricks 4 Kidz®, we believe that kids learn best through activities that engage their curiosity and creativity. The kids enjoy playing with LEGO® and don’t even realise it is Education!

We offer activities for children ages 3-13+:

  • After-School workshops; 
  • School Holiday Programs; 
  • Birthday Parties; 
  • School Incursions 
  • Pre-School Classes 

Start up support

  • Use of the Bricks 4 Kidz® logo and trademarks – the world’s fastest growing children's enrichment brand 
  • Dedicated, exclusive territory 
  • Support throughout the business building process

Training

  • Comprehensive four-day training, including marketing and sales strategies, in either Sydney or Melbourne
  • There are no training fees for new owners 
  • Access to systems and techniques, making business ownership simple and efficient

Ongoing support

  • Daily use of Yammer, an online collaboration tool for global owners
  • Weekly optional deep-dive calls for Australian owners focusing on topics for professional development
  • Quarterly Australian Owners Meetings, to share experiences and identify best practices
  • Annual BRICKS 4 KIDZ® Owners National Conference in Australia
  • Owners are always welcome to attend additional on-boarding training in Sydney or Melbourne
  • A well managed website that will promote your BRICKS 4 KIDZ® business at an easy to find web address to post all your programs, classes and other activities (vacation programs, birthday parties, etc.)
  • Continuing R&D at BRICKS 4 KIDZ® to develop and deploy new programs and products, reflecting new trends in the children services market

There are three elements to any successful franchise relationship: a great concept, a good location / territory and a great operator. We have the first element, and we’ll help you find the second. The real key is always the operator. As a Bricks 4 Kidz® Owner, YOU make all the difference. A franchisee that is willing to follow a proven education system, and is committed to excellence, fun, consistency and constant, positive communication with his / her kids and community will have great success. 

At Bricks 4 Kidz®, one of the world’s fastest growing franchises, students learn and have fun while building unique LEGO® creations! Explore the opportunity now.
